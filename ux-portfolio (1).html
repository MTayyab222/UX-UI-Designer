How I Work

A process built
for clarity.

Good design isn't accidental. It follows a rigorous, iterative process that keeps users at the centre and stakeholders informed at every stage.

Step one

Discover & Define

Stakeholder workshops, user interviews, and existing data analysis to align on problems worth solving. I synthesise findings into a clear design brief before any wireframe is drawn.

Step two

Information Architecture

Card sorting, tree testing, and site mapping to structure content and flows logically. Decisions here prevent costly redesigns later and ensure users always know where they are and where to go next.

Step three

Wireframe & Prototype

Low to high fidelity ‚Äî starting with pen-and-paper concepts, progressing to interactive Figma prototypes ready for usability testing. Iteration is fast and assumptions get tested early.

Step four

Test, Refine & Ship

Usability sessions with real users, synthesis of feedback into actionable design changes, and close collaboration with engineering through to launch. Post-ship monitoring ensures we hit the target metrics.
